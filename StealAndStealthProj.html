Steal And Stealth

"In Shadows We Fight, In Silence We Survive"

"Stealth and Steal" is a top-down 2D stealth-action puzzle game developed using Unity during a 486-hour internship at Lamina Studios, LLC. Set in a stylized, war-torn feudal Japan plagued by undead creatures, players take on the role of a loyal ninja under a noble Daimyo's command. Tasked with defending his domain, the ninja must complete a series of high-stakes missions-including rescues, stealth infiltrations, and escape operations while uncovering the dark origins of a mysterious curse. The game emphasizes stealth-based gameplay inspired by titles like Robbery Bob, featuring light and detection mechanics, limited-use weapons, and upgradable shadow skills such as teleportation and distraction clones. Players navigate through 20 story-driven levels divided into 3 acts, earning coins to upgrade abilities and tools. With a low-poly 3D cartoon art style, immersive sound design, and engaging enemy types like samurai, undead ghouls, and rival ninjas, "Stealth and Steal" delivers a challenging and atmospheric single-player experience. The project was collaboratively built using tools such as Unity, Blender, SourceTree, and Trello, and was developed by a multidisciplinary team under the name "The Noob Syndicate."
